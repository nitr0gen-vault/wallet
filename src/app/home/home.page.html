Nitr0gen Vault Welcome to Nitr0gen Wallet A new type of wallet security With Nitr0gen you have full control and access over your keys without the worry of losing them. Your crypto keys do not exist at any single location at rest or transit. On your device you have generated a OTK (One time key) which gives you control and access.
